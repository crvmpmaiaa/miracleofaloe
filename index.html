The science, quietly

Why aloe works.

One plant, 75+ active compounds. Here's what modern dermatology confirms about what your grandmother already knew.

  • 01 / Deep hydration

    Humectant mucopolysaccharides pull and hold water at the skin barrier.

    The reason aloe feels cooling the moment it lands — it's physically binding moisture in place.

  • 02 / Skin repair

    Polysaccharides and glycoproteins accelerate epithelial turnover.

    Faster cell replacement is why cracked skin looks and feels better in days, not weeks.

  • 03 / Antioxidant defence

    Vitamins A, C, E plus enzymes neutralise free-radical damage.

    The slow oxidation that dulls skin over the years — aloe is one of nature's quiet counterweights.

  • 04 / Anti-inflammatory

    Salicylic acid and anthraquinones calm redness and irritation.

    Soothing is not a marketing word here. It's a measured, repeatable effect.
